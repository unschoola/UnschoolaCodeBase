Welcome! I am SO glad that you found my site today.

This site, "Unschoola" i.e. schooling in your own way, began out of a desire to share from the years I have been teaching. As a child, I loved school, textbooks, and sitting in class listening to my teachers. I had ZERO idea that there were actually kids that struggled to understand the complex theorem and principle of textbook. As a kid, I always wanted to know how the airplane flies in the sky, How my fridge always keeps my ice-cream cold. How the bulb of my room glows. There are many other things which I didn't understand at that time. But have you ever thought if someone provides you the complete platform to understand this complex principle using animated video, full-time mentorship and a platform where you can make small working model and understand the principle behind it. Easy Right !!!. That is Us, we are a group of passionate professionals to make you understand. we offer online and offline courses to boost your academic. Complete mentorship Laboratory and play area to conduct the experiment and make small to large working model.
